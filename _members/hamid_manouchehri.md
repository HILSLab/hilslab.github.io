I am a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at the University at Buffalo with a background in electrical engineering, mechatronics, and robotics. My research interests include physical human-robot interaction, admittance control, robotic manipulation, and human-guided robot control. I work on designing, implementing, and evaluating robotic systems through control theory, experimental studies, simulation, and statistical analysis, with the long-term goal of developing practical robotic systems that can assist humans in real-world tasks.
