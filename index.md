# Human-in-the-Loop Systems Laboratory (HILS Lab)

The Human-in-the-Loop Systems Laboratory is a multidisciplinary research lab at the University at Buffalo directed by Dr. Ehsan Tarkesh Esfahani. Our work spans artificial intelligence, robotics, neuroscience, and human-centered automation, with current research in physical human-robot interaction, variable admittance control, compliant robotic grasping and manipulation, and human energy prediction models.
